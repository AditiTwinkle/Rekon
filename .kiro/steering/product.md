# Rekon - AI-Powered Audit and Compliance Platform

## Product Overview
Rekon is an AI-powered audit and compliance platform designed to automate regulatory compliance monitoring, audit preparation, and remediation guidance for financial institutions and enterprises.

## Core Value Proposition
- **Automated Compliance**: Reduces manual effort in regulatory analysis and audit preparation
- **Multi-Framework Support**: Integrates DORA, SOX, BMR, IOSCO, NIST, and other regulatory frameworks
- **AI-Powered Analysis**: Uses intelligent agents for requirement extraction, gap identification, and remediation guidance
- **Continuous Monitoring**: Provides real-time compliance status and alerts for regulatory changes

## Target Users
- Compliance officers in financial institutions
- IT security managers and application security engineers
- Regulatory affairs managers
- Audit managers and chief compliance officers
- Public company compliance teams
- Benchmark administrators

## Key Features
1. **Regulation Content Acquisition**: Automated fetching of latest regulatory requirements
2. **AI-Powered Checklist Generation**: Automatic extraction of testable compliance requirements
3. **Delta Analysis**: Comparison of organizational state against regulatory requirements
4. **Interactive Gap Assessment**: Intelligent questioning to discover hidden compliance gaps
5. **Remediation Guidance**: Actionable steps for closing compliance gaps
6. **Multi-Framework Dashboard**: Unified view of compliance status across all frameworks
7. **Evidence Management**: Collection and organization of compliance evidence
8. **Report Generation**: Comprehensive compliance reporting for management and regulators

## Regulatory Frameworks Supported
- **DORA (Digital Operational Resilience Act)**: Category A and B requirements
- **SOX (Sarbanes-Oxley Act)**: Financial reporting and internal controls
- **BMR (EU Benchmark Regulation)**: Financial benchmark administration
- **IOSCO Principles**: International securities regulation standards
- **NIST Cybersecurity Framework**: Federal cybersecurity requirements
- **AppHealth**: Application security and health compliance

## Business Impact
- Reduces compliance costs through automation
- Minimizes human error in compliance processes
- Ensures continuous compliance posture
- Provides proactive regulatory change monitoring
- Streamlines audit preparation and evidence collection
